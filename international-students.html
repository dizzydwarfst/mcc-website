International Students

Study in Vancouver with clearer guidance from inquiry to arrival

International students need more than broad promises. MCC uses this page to explain the real topics that matter: admission timing, study permit readiness, language expectations, and local student support.

Study permit planning Language readiness Vancouver student life

Before you apply

  • Compare programs and confirm your intended intake
  • Review general and program-specific requirements
  • Prepare passport and education documents early

Language expectations

  • MCC may review IELTS, TOEFL, CAEL, or equivalent evidence where appropriate
  • Language readiness should match the academic demands of your chosen program
  • Admissions can help you clarify which proof is suitable

Arrival planning

  • Plan enough time for visa, travel, and housing arrangements
  • Keep copies of key admissions and identity documents
  • Use contact support if you are unsure about next steps

Living in Vancouver

Study in a downtown location connected to transit and city life

MCC's Vancouver setting gives students access to transit, diverse communities, service-sector opportunities, and the daily rhythm of a major Canadian city. This page exists to help future students picture not just the classroom, but the full experience around it.
