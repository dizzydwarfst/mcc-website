Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to MCC?
Use our step-by-step application form on the Apply for Admissions page, then submit the required information and documents for review.
What are the admission requirements?
General requirements include academic readiness, identity documentation, and language readiness appropriate to the selected program.
Does MCC support international students?
Yes. International students can explore admissions guidance, document preparation, and Vancouver planning through our International Students page.
How can I estimate tuition?
Visit the Tuition, Fees & Payments page for planning guidance, then contact admissions for a program-specific estimate.
Can someone help me choose a program?
Absolutely. The admissions team can help you compare pathways, requirements, and next steps based on your goals.
