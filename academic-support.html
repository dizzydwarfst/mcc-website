Student Life / Academic & Learning Support

Stay on track — and get help the moment you need it

Academic success is one of the most important things students come to MCC for. Advisors and learning coaches are here for the planning conversations and the harder ones too.

Academic advisors Learning coaches Appeals & complaints
Academic Advisors

What an advisor can help with

Academic Advisors at MCC are here for the decisions that shape your study journey — not just emergencies.

Planning your studies

  • Course planning and sequencing
  • Understanding GPA and grading
  • Academic advancement opportunities

When things go off-plan

  • Addressing missed classes
  • Course withdrawal policies
  • Authorized Leave policy

If a dispute arises

  • Plagiarism charges
  • Grade appeals
  • Complaint procedures
Learning Support

Coaching and connections that build success

Academic coaching

Build the study habits, time management, and self-management skills that translate to better grades.

Difficulty support

Targeted help for students experiencing academic difficulty in a specific course or across a semester.

External professionals

When the right answer is outside MCC, advisors connect you with qualified external professionals.

Rights & responsibilities

Clarification of student rights and responsibilities so you know exactly where you stand.

How to Connect

Booking a meeting with an advisor

Visit the Front Desk or complete the Academic Advisor request form to book a meeting in person or online. Advisors aim to respond within one business day.
