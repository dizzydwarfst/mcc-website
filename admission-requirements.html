Admission Requirements

Ensure you meet the academic and language criteria before applying.

General Requirements

In general, applicants should be over 18 years old, have completed high school education, and have a good level of proficiency in the English language. International applicants must obtain a study permit for any study over 6 months.

In addition to satisfying the general requirements, applicants may also need to satisfy additional program requirements as listed below.

Program Specifics

Please visit the Interactive Course Catalog for detailed program descriptions.

Advanced Paralegal

(Without Co-op)
  • Completion of high school or mature student status (over 18 years old)
  • Evidence of IELTS 5.5 or equivalent; evidence of qualification can include an IELTS evaluation, or similar testing assessment to the level which can be closely deemed as equivalence (i.e. TOEFL iBT), or the student can take the College's language proficiency exam and achieve at least 70%.

Business Communications

Certificate Program
  • Generally, an IELTS 5.0 or equivalent, or by taking our college diagnostic test.
  • Students seeking bridging into diploma studies should also satisfy the requirement of the target diploma program before commencement.

Hospitality Operations

1-Year Diploma Program
  • At least 18 years of age
  • Evidence of IELTS 5.5 or equivalent; evidence of qualification can include an IELTS evaluation, or similar testing assessment.

Global Management in Hospitality

2-Year Diploma Program
  • At least 18 years of age
  • Evidence of IELTS 5.5 or equivalent; evidence of qualification can include an IELTS evaluation, or similar testing assessment.

International E-Sport Management

2-Year Diploma (Under Curriculum Approval)

(PROPOSED)

  • Completion of high school or mature student status (over 18 years old)
  • IELTS-A 5.5 with no band score lower than 5.0 (or equivalent TOEFL/CAEL)
  • Alternatively, students may take our Language Proficiency Test.
  • Completion of Business Communication program as entrance pre-requisite.
