Requirements

Review what you need before you apply

This page combines general admission expectations with program-specific guidance so students can quickly understand their readiness.

General entry guidance Language readiness support Program-specific expectations
Important note

Admissions may request additional documents if clarification is needed. If you are unsure whether a requirement applies to you, contact MCC before submitting your application.

General Requirements

What most applicants should expect

These guidelines apply across the MCC admissions experience before program-specific review begins.

Academic readiness

  • Completion of high school or an equivalent academic background
  • Mature students may be reviewed individually depending on program fit
  • Some programs may request additional evidence of readiness

Age and identity

  • Applicants are generally expected to be at least 18 years old
  • Government-issued identification is required as part of admissions review
  • International students should prepare passport documentation early

Language readiness

  • Applicants should be ready to study in English at the level required by their program
  • MCC may review IELTS, TOEFL, CAEL, or equivalent evidence where appropriate
  • Admissions can guide students who need clarification on acceptable proof
Program-Specific Guidance

Requirements by program area

These cards are designed to feel clearer and more current than the previous table layout while still giving students real guidance.

AI Microcredential

Designed for students and professionals ready for short-format, high-focus digital skills training.

  • Comfort with independent digital learning and project work
  • Basic computer literacy and reliable online access
  • English readiness for applied instruction and portfolio presentation

Caregiver Senior

Best suited to applicants preparing for people-focused training that emphasizes responsibility, communication, and practical care.

  • High school completion or equivalent background
  • English communication readiness for instruction and caregiving contexts
  • Preparedness for any practicum or placement-related expectations

Caregiver Infant

Ideal for students interested in early care environments and child-focused support training.

  • Academic readiness for structured diploma-level study
  • Communication skills suitable for care and safety-focused learning
  • Preparedness for any program placement or document requirements

Video & Content Creation

For students entering production, editing, storytelling, and digital media workflows.

  • Comfort with creative assignments and software-based project work
  • English readiness for collaboration, critique, and presentation
  • Interest in building a portfolio through applied work

Hospitality Operations Diploma

Strong fit for students preparing for service, operations, and customer-facing international hospitality environments.

  • High school completion or equivalent academic preparation
  • English readiness for service communication and classroom learning
  • Professional mindset suitable for team-based hospitality settings

International Esports Management Diploma

For students interested in a structured business and operations lens on esports, gaming, and event culture.

  • Academic readiness for diploma-level coursework
  • Communication and collaboration skills for team-based projects
  • Interest in management, events, media, and industry-facing work
