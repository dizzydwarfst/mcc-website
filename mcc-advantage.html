Four Pillars

The MCC Advantage in practice

Both our mission statement and educational objectives are driven by core values — based on the belief that every person must have effective access to lifelong learning.

1

Path to educational success

The college offers excellent educational opportunities in both vocational and academic studies — chosen so students can build careers in fields where Canada is hiring.

2

Eliminating barriers to career success

Course delivery is designed to benefit learners of all social and cultural backgrounds. Advising and support are part of the curriculum, not an afterthought.

3

Collegiality in learning

Courses are taught in a collegial atmosphere where consistent standards of integrity and educational competence are maintained at all times.

4

Effective access to lifelong learning

The college's essential mandate is to provide effective access to learners in every geographic and sectoral community — locally and internationally.

Small classes, real interaction

MCC keeps class sizes small so instructors know students by name and can adapt teaching to learner needs.

  • Direct instructor access
  • Hands-on, applied teaching
  • Cohort-based learning

Industry-aligned programs

An Advisory Board regularly reviews program competencies so what we teach stays current with what employers need.

  • Modern tools and software
  • Real-world projects
  • Pathways to degree programs

Designated and recognized

MCC is registered and designated by PTIB, recognized by BC EQA, and a Designated Learning Institution with IRCC.

  • PTIB designated
  • BC EQA recognized
  • DLI #O19329963492
