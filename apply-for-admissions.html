Start your journey today — in four guided steps

Application Guide

What to have ready

Four short steps. Here's what each one needs from you.

Step 1 — Personal info

Name, contact, citizenship, status in Canada, and your home address.

Step 2 — Agency contact

If an agent is applying on your behalf, share their details. Skip if not.

Step 3 — Program of choice

Pick your program, intake date, and available attendance options.

Step 4 — Upload documents

Passport or primary ID, permit/visa documents, academic records, language results, then sign and submit.
