Start your journey today — in four guided steps

Tell us about yourself, your agency (if any), the program you want to study, and upload your documents. Each step is simple, and we'll guide you through.

Four-step application Secure submission Advisor support available
Before you begin

Have your passport, study permit (if applicable), academic transcripts, and English-test results ready as PDF or image files (max 15 MB each).

Application Guide

What to have ready

Four short steps. Here's what each one needs from you.

Step 1 — Personal info

Name, contact, citizenship, status in Canada, and your home address.

Step 2 — Agency contact

If an agent is applying on your behalf, share their details. Skip if not.

Step 3 — Program of choice

Pick your program, intended semester, campus, and attendance mode.

Step 4 — Upload documents

Passport, study permit/visa, transcripts, English results, and signature.
