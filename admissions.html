Before you apply

We recommend that students review program fit, entry requirements, and tuition planning before beginning the application form.

  • Compare programs and intended career direction
  • Confirm academic and language eligibility
  • Prepare key documents before submission

For international students

International applicants should begin earlier to allow time for document preparation, offer processing, and study permit planning.

  • Passport and prior education documentation
  • English proficiency evidence where required
  • Planning for visa and arrival timelines
