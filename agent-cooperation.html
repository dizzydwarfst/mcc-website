Why Partner with MCC

What appointed agents receive

We back our recruitment partners with practical resources, ongoing communication, and a designated point of contact at the College.

Promotional materials

Updated brochures, program one-pagers, and recruiting collateral — including translated materials where available.

Training sessions

Live and on-demand training on programs, admission requirements, deadlines, and student support resources.

Campus tours

In-person or virtual tours of our Vancouver campus so you can speak from first-hand experience.

Ongoing updates

Regular communication about new programs, intake schedules, scholarships, and policy changes.

How to Become an MCC Agent

Three steps to get started

1

Submit the application

Complete the Appointed Agent Application form below and tell us about your agency, markets, and recruiting history.

2

Review & onboarding

Our team reviews each application individually. We'll set up an introductory call to discuss fit, target markets, and process.

3

Sign the agreement

Once we agree on terms and territory, we'll send a partnership agreement, marketing kit, and your dedicated agent contact.
