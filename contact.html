Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to MCC?
You can apply online through our Apply for Admissions page. Complete the step-by-step application form and submit the required documents. Our admissions team will review your application and respond within 5 business days.
What are the admission requirements?
General requirements include being over 18 years old, completion of high school education, and English language proficiency. Some programs have additional requirements. Visit our Admission Requirements page for full details.
Is MCC accredited?
Yes, Metropolitan Community College is accredited by the Private Training Institutions Branch (PTIB) of British Columbia and is designated under the BC Education Quality Assurance (EQA) framework.
What programs does MCC offer?
We offer six programs: AI Microcredential, Caregiver Senior, Caregiver Infant, Video & Content Creation, Hospitality Diploma, and Esports Diploma. Visit our Programs page to explore each one.
How much is tuition?
Tuition varies by program. Our Tuition, Fees & Payments page is currently being updated for the upcoming academic year. Please contact our admissions office for current pricing.
Does MCC accept international students?
Absolutely! We welcome international students from around the world. International applicants must obtain a study permit for programs longer than 6 months.
What English proficiency tests are accepted?
We accept IELTS, TOEFL iBT, CAEL, and equivalent assessments. Students may also take the College's own language proficiency exam. Required scores vary by program.
How do I get a study permit?
After receiving your official Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from MCC, you can apply for a study permit through Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC). We recommend starting this process early.
Can I transfer credits from another institution?
Transfer credit evaluations are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our admissions office with your transcripts for an assessment.
How do I contact the admissions office?
You can reach us by phone at +1 (604) 555-0100, by email at admissions@mcc.edu, or by filling out the contact form on this page. Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM PST.
