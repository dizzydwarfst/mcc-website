About the Program

What is CELPIP Preparation?

The CELPIP Preparation Program offers in-depth strategies and skill-building for each component of the CELPIP-General Test. The curriculum was developed in collaboration with the program administrator and is delivered by experienced instructors. With the right combination of student effort and structured practice, most learners achieve a 1–2 level improvement in their CELPIP score.

Two Tracks

Pick the schedule that fits your timeline

Intense Program · 3 weeks

CAD 1,450

4 hours per day, 5 days a week. Designed for students with a near-term test date who want a focused sprint.

Regular Program · 12 weeks

CAD 2,800

2 hours per day, 5 days a week. A steadier pace for students who want time to absorb each section thoroughly.

Course materials: CAD 125 · Note: this program is not eligible for a Post-Graduation Work Permit.

What You'll Practise

All four CELPIP components

Listening

  • Conversations and problem-solving
  • News and academic talks
  • Note-taking strategies

Reading

  • Correspondence and applied reading
  • Information passages
  • Viewpoints and inference

Writing

  • Email task framework
  • Survey response structure
  • Tone, accuracy, and timing

Speaking

  • All 8 speaking task types
  • Pronunciation and pacing
  • Confidence under timing pressure

Admission

Either of the following will satisfy the placement requirement:

  • Previous CELPIP test score
  • CELPIP diagnostic test taken at the College

Who it's for

  • Permanent residence applicants
  • Citizenship applicants
  • Professional licensing candidates
  • Anyone aiming to lift their CLB level

What you'll get

  • Targeted instruction by section
  • Practice-test feedback
  • Test-day strategy and timing drills
  • Course materials package
