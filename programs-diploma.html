Student Voices

Hear from our graduates

International students from around the world share what studying at MCC meant for them.

“

I came in knowing nothing about AI workflows. By the end of the microcredential I had shipped a working prototype that translates voice notes from Hindi to English using three different models chained together. My cohort instructor helped me turn it into a portfolio piece I actually use in interviews.

Aarav Sharma

🇮🇳 India · AI Microcredential

“

Back home I looked after my grandmother for years, but the Canadian standards for senior care are very different. MCC taught me the paperwork, the dignity side, the safe transfer techniques — everything a BC care home actually expects. I started my placement three weeks after graduation.

Grace Okonkwo

🇳🇬 Nigeria · Caregiver Senior

“

I had experience with children but no formal certification. The Caregiver Infant program gave me the early-childhood theory and the hands-on hours I needed. The practicum placement with a local daycare is what really sealed it — they offered me a position before I finished the diploma.

Mei-Ling Tan

🇵🇭 Philippines · Caregiver Infant

“

I was editing wedding videos on my phone when I applied. Now I have a short-film reel, client work for two Vancouver cafes, and a YouTube channel the instructors pushed me to launch. The program treats you like a working creator from day one, not like a student waiting to graduate.

Luiz Ferreira

🇧🇷 Brazil · Video & Content Creation

“

The service industry in Vancouver moves fast and guests expect a lot. MCC ran mock check-ins, complaint scenarios, even a full banquet service simulation. I walked into my first front-desk shift at a downtown hotel two weeks after my final exam and nothing surprised me.

Sofía Álvarez

🇨🇴 Colombia · Hospitality Diploma

“

My parents wanted me to study business. I wanted to work in esports. MCC was the only school that treated gaming as a real industry — event ops, team management, sponsorship decks, broadcast basics. I now coordinate regional tournaments for a Canadian org and my family finally gets it.

Kenji Tanaka

🇯🇵 Japan · Esports Diploma
