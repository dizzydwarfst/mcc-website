TCF Canada Preparation Program Officially Launches
MCC's new TCF Canada Preparation Program launches this month, completing our French certification suite alongside TEF Canada Prep.
Read More

Hello with crisp winter greetings from MCC. This March marks another milestone in our French-language journey: the official launch of the TCF Canada Preparation Program. Joining our well-established TEF Canada Prep, the new TCF program now gives our students complete coverage of the two main federally recognized French assessments for immigration and academic admission.

The program is built around the four TCF components - reading, listening, writing, and speaking - with simulation-heavy workshops and feedback from instructors with examiner experience. Students may now choose the assessment that best matches their goals, or prepare for both, depending on where they are applying and what scores they need.

Student Journey: Léa's two-exam strategy. Léa, a Niveau 4 graduate from our 2025 cohort, is preparing for both TEF and TCF this spring. Her academic path requires TCF, but her permanent-residency application accepts TEF. "Doing both feels heavy," she admitted, "but my advisor mapped the overlap. Most of the practice is the same. I just sit two exams instead of one."

Student Journey: Hossein's quiet climb. Hossein joined Niveau 1 in early 2025 with no prior French at all. He is now sitting in the inaugural TCF Prep cohort. "Two years ago I could not order coffee in French. This week I wrote a 200-word formal letter in a mock exam. The progress sneaks up on you here."

This launch closes a multi-year arc that began with our 2024 Alliance Francaise partnership. From Niveau 1 through C1-level mastery, the pathway is now genuinely complete - and the students walking through it are proof.

Hello, with great pride, from the entire MCC team. This October, we proudly launched our newest academic offering: the TEF Canada Preparation Program.
