MCC kicks off 2026 with its inaugural French Cultural Week — a week of Francophone film screenings, guest speakers from Quebec, French cooking workshops, and a campus-wide celebration of Francophonie.
Read More

January marked a historic moment at Metropolitan Community College as we proudly hosted our inaugural French Cultural Week. An immersive celebration of Francophonie, the event week transformed our campus and brought our expansive French curriculum to life beyond the classroom walls.

Organized in collaboration with Alliance Française Vancouver, the week featured a rich tapestry of activities. Students enjoyed screenings of award-winning Francophone films, attended specialized French culinary workshops, and joined highly engaging conversational cafes. Guest speakers from Quebec's hospitality sector also joined us virtually, sharing insights on the cultural nuances of bilingual customer service.

The unquestionable highlight was the student showcase. Learners from the ESL French, French Hospitality, and TEF Prep programs presented collaborative projects entirely in French. It was an inspiring testament to how far MCC has come since we first integrated French language education into our core disciplines. The week concluded with a campus-wide reception featuring authentic French cuisine, ending our first month of 2026 on a soaring, celebratory note.

October 2025
Issue #34
🇫🇷 TEF Preparation Program Officially Launches
MCC introduces its new TEF Preparation Program, designed to help students achieve certification for immigration, university admission, and professional opportunities.
Read More

This October, Metropolitan Community College proudly officially launched its newest academic offering: the TEF (Test d'évaluation de français) Preparation Program. This marks the culmination of months of meticulous curriculum design, establishing MCC as a premier destination for formal French assessment preparation on the West Coast.

Tailored for students targeting immigration to Francophone regions, federal employment, or admission to Francophone universities, the program provides comprehensive training across all four crucial components of the TEF exam: reading, listening, writing, and speaking. Our expert instructors, many of whom have experience as official examiners, utilize targeted simulations and intensive workshops to build essential test-taking strategies.

With both 8-week intensive and 12-week standard formats available, the TEF Prep Program operates in powerful synergy with our existing ESL French suites (A1–C1). This launch ensures that our students are not only learning French, but are expertly equipped to translate their bilingualism into recognized credentials and tangible, life-changing opportunities.

MCC proudly launches its new French Hospitality Program in partnership with leading hotels in Montreal. Students will study hospitality operations with a French-language focus.
Read More

March 2025 marks a transformative milestone for Metropolitan Community College with the much-anticipated launch of our French Hospitality Diploma. Merging elite hospitality management training with comprehensive Francophone cultural and linguistic immersion, this program is designed to forge the next generation of bilingual industry leaders.

The successful launch of this program underscores MCC's innovative approach to bridging niche academic pathways with direct, high-trajectory employment.

Issue #21
🇫🇷 Full ESL French Program Launches — All Levels A1 to C1
MCC officially launches its comprehensive ESL French Program covering all proficiency levels from A1 (Beginner) through C1 (Advanced).
Read More

It is with immense pride that we announce the official opening of the Metropolitan Community College comprehensive ESL French Program. A watershed moment for our academic offerings, this program provides a meticulously structured, full-spectrum language journey covering every level from A1 (Beginner) through C1 (Advanced).

Designed with absolute immersion in mind, the program caters to everyone from complete novices to those seeking professional and academic fluency. Our curriculum progresses rapidly: from basic vocabulary and daily routines in A1/A2, moving into complex workplace communication and current events in B1/B2, and culminating in the rigorous literary analysis and formal presentations required at the C1 level.

Classes are offered in highly flexible morning and evening blocks, accessible to both full-time learners and working professionals. By equipping our graduates with a commanding grasp of the French language, MCC is actively opening pathways to federal employment, enhanced immigration profiles, and broader sociolinguistic integration across Canada.

🇫🇷 MCC Partners with Alliance Française Vancouver

Read More

Metropolitan Community College is honored to announce the signing of a strategic partnership with Alliance Française Vancouver. This landmark agreement represents a fundamental pillar in our vision to bring premier French-language education and robust cultural engagement to our student body.

With its deep-rooted legacy of promoting Francophone culture globally, the Alliance Française will be instrumental in enriching our upcoming curriculums. Through this partnership, MCC students will gain unprecedented access to premium, authentic DELF/DALF exam preparation resources. Furthermore, the agreement initiates an exciting exchange of expert instructors and guest lecturers, ensuring our classrooms are infused with vibrant, native linguistic expertise.

Beyond academics, this collaboration will also bring vibrant cultural programming to the MCC campus, from film screenings to art exhibits. This vital partnership actively lays the essential groundwork for our highly anticipated, comprehensive ESL French Program slated to launch this fall.
