Student Journey: The Niveau 1 founding twenty. Our first Niveau 1 cohort - the one that walked into a brand-new classroom in March 2024 - became something close to a family this year. We are following this group as they finish their advanced French in 2025.

Looking ahead, 2025 will be about depth, not launches: cementing the bilingual pathway, growing the cohort sizes, and producing the first MCC-trained TEF and TCF graduates. Thank you for being part of this journey.

June 27, 2024
TEF & TCF Canada Prep Launch - Complete French Suite Now Live

Read More

Hello, with great pride, from the entire MCC team. June 2024 closes one of the most ambitious six-month rollouts in MCC's history. The TEF Canada Preparation Program and the TCF Canada Preparation Program both launch this month, completing our French-language suite alongside ESL French Niveaux 1 through 4 and the French Hospitality Diploma.

From January's announcement to June's certification programs, the entire pathway came online in six months. Students entering MCC today can move from absolute-beginner French through advanced fluency, layer in hospitality industry training, and prepare for the federally recognized TEF or TCF assessments - all under one roof.

Student Journey: Anjali's reason for being here. Anjali enrolled in the inaugural TEF Prep cohort with one goal: enough CRS points to bring her family to Canada. "Every word I learn is for them," she said in our first-week interview. "I am not studying French because I love it. I am studying it so my parents can finally visit." The team finds her work ethic moves them every single class.

Student Journey: Léa's two-exam strategy. Léa, a Niveau 2 student from our March cohort, signed up for both TEF and TCF prep on day one. Her academic path requires TCF, but her permanent-residency application accepts TEF. "Doing both feels heavy," she admitted, "but my advisor mapped the overlap. Most of the practice is the same. I just sit two exams instead of one."

Both 8-week intensive and 12-week standard formats are available. The instructors - many of them with direct experience as official examiners - have been preparing for this launch since the day the Alliance Francaise partnership was signed back in February.

None of them had ever been to Canada before. His mother held his diploma in both hands during the photo and refused to put it down for almost an hour. Daniel told us afterwards: "Today my mother understands what I have been doing here for two years."

Student Journey: Anna's valedictorian speech. Anna, our first valedictorian, spent two weeks writing her speech and threw it away the night before. She started over with a single sentence: "We are the cohort that did not know if there would be a second cohort." Her speech was four minutes long. There was not a dry eye on stage. Two years later, that line is still on the wall outside the academic office.

January 24, 2023
Welcome to Metropolitan Community College - Our First Newsletter
The very first MCC newsletter introduces our mission, our founding programs, and the cohort about to begin classes.
Read More

Dear MCC family, welcome to the very first issue of the Metropolitan Community College newsletter. With our doors newly opened on Water Street, we are thrilled to begin documenting the story of this college - month by month, milestone by milestone.

Our founding programs are accepting their first learners: Next-Gen Applied AI Mastery, Caregiver Senior, Caregiver Infant, Video Content Creation and Social Media Management, Hospitality Operations Diploma, and International Esports Management Diploma, alongside our full ESL pathway. The campus is humming with possibility, and the first students arrive in just a few short weeks.

Student Journey: The first registration on day one. The very first registration came in at 9:04 a.m. on opening day. Her name is Tanvi, and she signed up for the Hospitality Operations Diploma. She told us she had been refreshing the registration page since 8:55. "I wanted to be the first," she said simply. "I wanted to be able to tell my children I was the first." She is. The certificate confirming it is framed in our admissions office.

Student Journey: A founding student writing the first email. By 11 a.m. that same day, an email arrived from Mateo, a Next-Gen Applied AI Mastery applicant: "I have been waiting for a school like this for two years. I will be there in January. Tell me what to bring." We told him a notebook and an open mind. He brought both.

Expect monthly updates: program news, milestones, and the quiet wins from our community. Thank you for being here at the very beginning.
