Our Mission

A commitment to lifelong learning

At Metropolitan Community College, we are committed to equipping students with the critical thinking skills, resourcefulness and knowledge that add value to themselves, their families and the community. We bring together the latest technologies, diverse cultural backgrounds and a dynamic learning environment to identify solutions to the complex global problems of the 21st century.

What it looks like in practice

How the mission shapes the MCC experience

Critical thinking first

Programs are taught to develop reasoning, judgement, and problem solving — not just credentials.

Resourceful graduates

We prepare students to navigate change, learn new tools, and adapt to fast-moving industries.

Value to community

Our graduates carry their learning into workplaces, families, and the wider community in Vancouver and beyond.

Latest technologies

From AI tooling to broadcast workflows to industry software, programs reflect what employers actually use today.

Diverse cultural backgrounds

Students from across the world learn alongside Canadians, sharing perspectives that strengthen every classroom discussion.
