About Metropolitan Community College

Empowering students through innovation, care, and excellence since 2023.

Our Mission

Metropolitan Community College is dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality education that prepares students for meaningful careers in a rapidly evolving world. We combine practical skills training with innovative teaching methodologies to ensure every graduate is workforce-ready.

Our Vision

To be British Columbia's most innovative community college — a place where diverse learners from around the world come to discover their passions, build professional skills, and launch careers that make a difference.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We set high standards in everything we do, from curriculum design to student support.

Diversity

We celebrate the richness of our multicultural community and provide an inclusive learning environment.

Innovation

We embrace emerging technologies and teaching methods to stay ahead of industry needs.

Care

We treat every student as an individual, providing personalized guidance and support throughout their journey.
