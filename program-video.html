Programs / Diploma / Video Content Creation and Social Media Management

Video Content Creation and Social Media Management Diploma

Learn to plan, shoot, edit, and publish content the way working creators and Vancouver studios actually do it. Build a real portfolio while you study.

40 weeks Studio access PTIB accredited
About the Program

What is the Video Content Creation and Social Media Management Diploma?

This diploma is built for the modern creator economy. Students learn cinematography, editing, motion graphics, storytelling, and platform-native publishing across short-form, long-form, and branded content. The curriculum mirrors how Vancouver-based studios, agencies, and creators actually work — from concept to upload.

Graduates leave with a multi-piece portfolio, real client experience, and a working understanding of the business side of being a creator.

Why It Matters

Why creator skills, why now?

Video is the dominant medium

Short-form video is now the primary marketing channel for most consumer brands. Every business needs people who can produce it well.

Vancouver is Hollywood North

BC hosts one of the largest film and digital media industries in North America — with continuous demand for skilled production crews.

The creator economy is real

Independent creators, freelance editors, and content strategists earn full-time incomes. The gap is access to skill, not opportunity.

Earning Potential

What graduates earn in Canada

Salary ranges reflect typical Canadian market rates as of 2026.

Entry Level

$45K – $58K

First role within 12 months. Junior editor, social content producer, studio runner.

Mid Career

$62K – $90K

Two to four years in. Senior editor, content lead, in-house producer.

Senior & Specialist

$95K – $140K+

Five+ years or successful freelance practice. Director of content, post-production lead, founder.

Curriculum

What you'll study

Module 1 · Visual Storytelling

  • Story structure and narrative arcs
  • Shot composition and framing
  • Pacing and emotional beats
  • Pre-production and storyboarding

Module 2 · Cinematography

  • Camera fundamentals (mirrorless, cinema, phone)
  • Lighting for interview, narrative, and product
  • Audio capture and field recording
  • On-set workflow and roles

Module 3 · Editing & Post

  • Premiere Pro / DaVinci Resolve
  • Colour correction and grading
  • Sound design and mixing
  • Motion graphics in After Effects

Module 4 · Platform-Native Content

  • YouTube long-form strategy
  • TikTok and Reels short-form
  • Thumbnails, hooks, and retention
  • AI tools in the creator workflow

Module 5 · Business of Content

  • Pricing freelance work
  • Contracts and copyright in BC
  • Client communication and pitching
  • Building a brand and audience

Capstone · Portfolio & Showcase

  • Produce a short-film reel
  • Complete one paid client project
  • Public showcase event
  • Industry mentor critique
