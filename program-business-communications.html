Business Communication Certificate

Practical business communication training for non-native English speakers — polish the writing, presentation, and public speaking skills that matter at work.

8 weeks full-time 2 courses · 6 credits Certificate
About the Program

What is the Business Communication Certificate?

The Business Communication program enhances students' communication skills in a business environment. Two intensive courses help students upgrade their business writing skills along with their presentation and public speaking skills — both essential to succeed in today's complex business environment.

It also serves as a bridging option for students whose English needs to reach the level required for entry into MCC's diploma programs.

Curriculum

What you'll study

Course 1 · Business Writing

Email tone, formal documents, reports, and the writing patterns expected in workplace contexts across industries.

Course 2 · Presentation & Public Speaking

Structuring a presentation, handling questions, group facilitation, and presenting confidently in English.

Who It Is For

A program built for two journeys

Working professionals

Newcomers and international workers who already have strong industry experience but want to communicate more confidently in English at work.

Future MCC diploma students

Students preparing to bridge into a Hospitality, Esports, or other MCC diploma whose English needs to reach the program-entry level.

Career-changers

Anyone moving into a customer-facing or office-based role and wanting workplace English skills mapped to real business tasks.

Program details

  • Duration: 8 full-time weeks
  • 2 courses · 6 credits · 160 contact hours
  • In-class delivery at our Vancouver campus
  • Small cohorts with direct instructor feedback

Admission

  • Generally an IELTS 4.0 or equivalent
  • A college diagnostic test may be used for placement
  • No upper-secondary diploma required to enrol
  • Bridging students must meet diploma requirements separately

Tuition & fees

  • Application fee: CAD 200
  • Tuition: CAD 3,900
  • Course materials: CAD 100
  • Payment plans available — speak with admissions
